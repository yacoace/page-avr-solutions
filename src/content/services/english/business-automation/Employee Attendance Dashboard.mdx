The Employee Attendance Dashboard is designed to efficiently monitor and analyze attendance records. Using Power BI, it provides insights such as clock-in and clock-out times, hours worked per day, week, month, and year, as well as absences and their reasons. You can also filter data by individual or department for more tailored analysis.

This service is ideal for HR and management teams, helping identify attendance patterns, calculate overtime or underperformance, and evaluate compliance. With this tool, you can manage attendance proactively and strategically.
